SUBVERSITYPE

Abstract typeface and specimen booklet: A critique of institutional messaging.
February 2026

SUBVERSITYPE is an abstract, illegible typeface meant to undermine the illusion of authenticity in institutional messages. It is designed for institutions that speak without caring to be understood. This project undermines systematic methods of conveying information, reflecting how institutions “provide” information but do not prioritize comprehension.

Each glyph is built from an equal number of bars on a grid; bar height varies to create the letterforms. The typeafe was designed and developed in Adobe Illustrator, and optimized for download in Glyphs. The specimen booklet uses SUBVERSITYPE to set the OCADU Academic Policies in paragraphs of dense text: hundreds of pages of policies that students are expected to know, yet OCAD doesn’t make these documents easy to find, read, and memorize.

This typeface and specimen booklet serve as a critique of institutional messaging, anda commentary on the future potential of type legibility.

Tools Used:

  • Adobe InDesign
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Glyphs
