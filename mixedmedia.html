Why Do You Love Independence?

Mixed media interview video with accompanying documentation book.
2024

'Why do you love Independence?' was the question asked to three interviewees. Their answers were recorded and used as the audio track to guide the video. The visual content of the video was made by converting found footage into individual frames. The frames were then printed and manipulated manually using oil pastels, colored pencils, and other analogue materials. Once finished, the frames were scanned at a high resolution and reformatted back into video. The result is a frame-by-frame animation combining digital and analogue elements.

The accompanying documentation book contains the official interview transcript along with process documentation. The background music was created by artist Vraell, who I reached out to directly to collaborate with me on this project.

Tools Used:

  • Adobe After Effects
  • Adobe Audition
  • Adobe InDesign
  • Oil Pastels
